About Us

Formed in 2016, Mega Sports Management Group (MMG) is a multi-faceted company that provides contract negotiations, financial management, marketing and endorsement programs for professional athletes and performing arts artists. We feel that the athletic and recreation industries are at their most vulnerable states in Kenya.  We will work with you to create and implement a representation program that provides security to successfully participate in the world of professional sport and music through a variety of services which we offer to our potential clients. Mega Sports Management Group is actively working with brands and corporations across Kenya to create the ultimate working conditions and unique smart business solutions to catapult all of our clients into the top most achievable heights in their careers.

COMPANY GOALS & OBJECTIVES

Recognizing how important it is for you to have experienced representation, we have assembled a unique team of highly qualified professionals to assist you in fulfilling your dreams of becoming a professional athlete/artist/video jockey.

Our Mission is to provide ready equipped solutions to the Clients and maximize their financial outcomes. Our emphasis is to establish a representation program that maximizes your earning potential for both the long and short term. We are confident that with the proper guidance and support, you will be able to enjoy a long professional career.
